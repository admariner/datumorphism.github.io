Jaccard index is the ratio of the size of the intersect of the set and the size of the union of the set.

$$
J(A, B) = \frac{ \vert A \cap B \vert }{ \vert A \cup B \vert }
$$

Jaccard distance $d_J(A,B)$ is defined as

$$
d_J(A,B) = 1 - J(A,B).
$$
